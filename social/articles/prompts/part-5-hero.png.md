## Part 5 — Production Ready / Hero Image

**Aspect ratio:** 1200×630

**Concept:** A horizontal roadmap with 5 stages: Prototype → Schema → Validation → Plugins → Production. Each stage is a circular station connected by a path that graduates from dashed line (early stages) to solid line (production). Journey/progression visual.

**Logo:** Top-left corner. ctrodb wordmark (user uploads the logo file separately).

**Composition:**
- Full background: dark warm gradient (#1e293b at top → #0f172a at bottom) to suggest a journey beginning in uncertainty and ending in solid ground
- Center: 5 stations equally spaced horizontally across the full width (about 180px apart)
  - Each station: a circle, 72px diameter, with a simple line icon inside
  - Station 1 (left): dashed outline circle (#94a3b8), icon: pencil/pen sketch line in slate-400
  - Station 2: dashed outline (#94a3b8), icon: document/lines in slate-300
  - Station 3: half-dashed half-solid circle, icon: shield in amber (#f59e0b)
  - Station 4: solid outline (#06b6d4), icon: puzzle piece in cyan
  - Station 5 (right): solid outline (#06b6d4), filled center (#06b6d4 at 20%), icon: checkmark shield in cyan (#06b6d4)
  - Below each station: label in 12px sans-serif. "Prototype", "Schema", "Validation", "Plugins", "Production" — the last label is in bold cyan
- Connecting path between stations: a horizontal line at the center height of the circles
  - Between 1-2: dashed (#64748b), 2px
  - Between 2-3: dashed transitioning to solid at midpoint
  - Between 3-4: solid (#06b6d4 at 60%), 2px
  - Between 4-5: solid (#06b6d4), 2px
- Below the roadmap (60px gap): small text "Part 5 of 5" centered in slate-500, 12px
- Subtle in the background (very faint): a thin dotted arc suggesting a path that curves upward from left to right (success trajectory)

**Colors:** Background dark warm gradient (#1e293b → #0f172a), dashed stations #94a3b8, solid stations cyan #06b6d4, filled station cyan at 20%, icons slate-400/amber-300/cyan, path lines #64748b / #06b6d4, labels #cbd5e1 / last label bold #06b6d4, "Part 5 of 5" #64748b.

**Typography:** Station labels in 13px sans-serif, "Part 5 of 5" in 12px sans-serif. No headline needed — the roadmap tells the story.

**Technical notes:** 1200×630. Logo top-left (user uploads separately). Icons should be simple single-stroke line art, not detailed illustrations. The success trajectory arc in the background should be barely visible — the reader shouldn't notice it consciously, it's a subliminal upward curve. The progression from dashed to solid is the main visual metaphor.
